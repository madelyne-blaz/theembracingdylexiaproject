My Mission

Three pillars. One purpose.

Everything I do comes back to these three commitments.

Advocate

I speak up for the 1 in 5 people with dyslexia who are too often overlooked, misdiagnosed, or told to work harder — instead of smarter.

Support

Through dyslexia reading kits and real-world resources, I give teachers, parents, and students the tools they need to thrive.

Teach

Knowing the warning signs changes everything. I'm committed to spreading that knowledge — in classrooms, communities, and online.
