Common Questions

How do I request a reading kit?

Fill out the form and select "Request a Reading Kit" I'll follow up with details!

Are your resources really free?

Yes! All of my guides and downloads are completely free. My mission is to make dyslexia support accessible to everyone.

Can you speak at my school or event?

I'd love to! I am based in Washington and Utah, regardless of where your event is, I would love to collaborate! Reach out through the form and let me know about your event. I speak to classrooms, parent groups, and community organizations, in person or through video calls.

I do my best to reply within a few days.
